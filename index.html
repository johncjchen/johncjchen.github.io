Introduction

I am a second-year mechanical engineering major at UC Santa Barbara. My current interests lie in the fields of mechatronics and renewable energies, but I am very open to all other aspects of the mechanical engineering discipline.

Although I am a ME major, I love programming and all things related. I consider computer science my side passion and look forward to gaining the knowledge and experience necessary begin developing my own apps and bots. As you may be able to tell, my website makes use of JS, HTML, and CSS.

My third great interest is with machine learning of any type. For the past year, I have been enamored with the concept of computer vision and its various aspects, such as image classification, object tracking, and depth perception. Both MATLAB and Python have provided great outlets for experimenting with ML.
